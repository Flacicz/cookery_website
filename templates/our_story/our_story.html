Passion for Baking

At WarmBread Boutique Bakery, we apply time-tested balanced methods that offer unique flavors and textures. Our range of hand-crafted bread includes varieties from all over the world and is designed according to high-quality industry standards. We use our natural sourdough in each type of our bread. We never take shortcuts for our processes since that is how we achieve that irresistible freshness and taste.

Our Story

WarmBread Bakery was founded in Autumn, 2016

The idea of creating WarmBread came about when we lived and worked as bakers in Chicago, Illinois. Our story starts with us, with how we met, what we did and learned, and now you are a part of it.
Our very first meeting at our BA in Culinary Arts in 2011 marked the beginning of our journey. We were sitting next to each other at the open baking workshop and have not left each other ever since. WarmBread is the apex of eight years of work as cooks who have now become bakers – collecting innovative and creative ideas, sharing skills, food recipes, and our true passion to feed people with the food made with love and care. While in Chicago, we acquired priceless skills and practice by working closely with the best bakers in the industry. Jane worked under the direction of Nicholas Janko at TG Bread&Butter, and Charlotte joined the Craftsmen&Wolves crew led by Willy Burner and Elizabeth Ballock.
